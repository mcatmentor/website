Our Mission

Preparing for the MCAT is both stressful and financially burdensome. In response, we founded MCAT Mentor: a nonprofit initiative dedicated to expanding access to MCAT preparation.
We're developing a free online platform for future generations of pre-meds - the platform we wish we had: comprehensive, intuitive, and entirely free. Our goal is to level the playing field for students from underserved backgrounds by removing financial barriers to pursuing medicine.
MCAT Mentor aims to empower students based on merit, not money.
